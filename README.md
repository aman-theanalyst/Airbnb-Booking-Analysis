## üîç Major Insights

### 1. Demand Concentration  
- Manhattan and Brooklyn dominate in both listings and guest demand.

### 2. Pricing Variability  
- Manhattan has the highest average nightly prices.  
- Outer boroughs show more affordable pricing but stable demand.

### 3. High-Performance Neighborhoods  
- Williamsburg, Bushwick, Bedford-Stuyvesant, Harlem, and Upper West Side show strong host activity and guest interest.

### 4. Room Type Preferences  
- Entire homes/apartments and private rooms capture most bookings.  
- Shared rooms perform poorly.

### 5. Short-Term Stay Trend  
- Most listings cater to short-term stays (<30 nights).

### 6. Host Competition  
- A few hosts manage multiple listings, increasing market competition.

### 7. Queens Airport Effect  
- Neighborhoods near airports show higher review counts due to convenience for travelers.
